A collection of useful tools and resources for people playing Star Wars™ Galaxy of Heroes

Tools

Shard/Gear Farming Calculator
A tool that shows you the best way to farm a toon's shards and gear and gives an estimate of the amount of time it will take to do so.

Ship Farming Calculator
A tool that shows you the best way to farm a ship's shards and gives an estimate of the amount of time it will take to do so.

Crystal Farming Guide
Shows you where to get crystals for free and how many you can get per day.

Mod Farming Guide
Explains how mods work and shows you the drop rates for the different mod varieties.
